Company HR Policies and Procedures

1. Leave Policies
---------------
Annual Leave:
- Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance
- Maximum 10 days can be carried forward to the next year

Sick Leave:
- 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- Must notify supervisor within 2 hours of work start time
- Medical certificate required for absences of 2 or more days
- Sick leave can be taken in half-day increments

Parental Leave:
- 16 weeks paid maternity leave
- 2 weeks paid paternity leave
- Must give 3 months notice before starting leave

2. Working Hours
--------------
- Standard work week is 40 hours
- Core hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- Flexible start time between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM
- Overtime must be pre-approved by supervisor

3. Remote Work Policy
------------------
- Eligible employees can work remotely up to 2 days per week
- Must maintain core hours during remote work
- Need reliable internet connection and quiet workspace
- Equipment will be provided by company

4. Code of Conduct
---------------
- Treat all colleagues with respect
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Report harassment or discrimination immediately
- Follow safety and security protocols

5. Performance Reviews
-------------------
- Conducted twice yearly (June and December)
- Self-assessment required
- Goals and objectives set for next period
- Salary reviews linked to performance
